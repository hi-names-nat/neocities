Netscape Cybermind

I love this game! Originally developed by a team I was unfortunately not a part of for 7DFPS, a 7 day FPS game jam, the lead developer and visionary Josh Wilson decided to continue working on the game and ideally eventually release it as a finished product. During that time, he enlisted me to at first only level design, but eventually complexify the entirety of the game, including improvements to the games basics.

The game at its core is inspired by Half-Life and Y2K aesthetics. I love both of these things (obviously, you're on my personal neocities site after all) so I was very enthused about the game and its concept. I even came up with some incredibly core world and level designs that I'm excited for people to eventually see!! :3

I can't speak much about anything else. I don't think Josh would be too happy with me. However the 7DFPS version (with some improvements by yours truly) is available down below. You can also click here to see something of Josh's that I think about constantly, everyday, every single day of my life no break. (I love Dr. DialUp I love him i love him AA AAA )

Development is still in progress! I have not contibuted to the game as much recently, but I plan to eventually get back into it. It is a project I believe in and it's going to be so exciting to see it finally out there, one day.
