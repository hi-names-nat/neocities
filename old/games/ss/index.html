Silicon Starship

I can't talk much about this yet, it's not super far along yet, but my parter and I are currently working on a prototype that I'll put here (and only here) when it's ready (aren't you so lucky).

Silicon Starship is a game that blends platformer and metroidvania elements. The player will explore large, Banjo-Tooie style connected areas and collect Scrap to build new abilities for their player character, a Robot named Sil.

Once the prototype is here I'll do a better writeup and go over the intended story and etc.

Slicon Starship started on Godot, but it moved to Unreal Engine because I could not get Godot working in a way I was happy with.

No downloads or anything yet, sorry.
